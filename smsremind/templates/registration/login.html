Technology

{% if 0 %}

MetroBill is a Cloud based Invoicing application Delivered as a Service (SAAS), This means you get power of cloud computing without any worries related to Servers, Hardware, Patches, Maintainance and Upgrades.
Your applications is maintained by us and any upgrades, patches and new software features are automatically applied.
Your Data and Passwords are Encrypted with latest technologies and stored in Secured Datacenters Owned and Managed by HP

MetroBill is powered by HP Cloud Services which uses open cloud architecture based on OpenStack™ HP's Converged Cloud framework which provides the values of choice, confidence, and consistency.

An open and interoperable approach to cloud infrastructure and services provides the foundation for creating the next generation of cloud-based services. By closely collaborating with the developer community and capitalizing on the strength of HP's technology portfolio, HP Cloud Services is committed to providing seamless and secure experiences for every customer

Data Privacy

{% if 0 %}

Given the ever growing popularity of software as a service, many people are rightly concerned about privacy. Many large corporations are frequently in the news for changes made to their privacy policies.

We respect your privacy and we assure you that your data will never be shared with third-parties and It will not be used for any other purposes besides your own usage.
